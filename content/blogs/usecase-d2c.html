The D2C Packaging Moment

For direct-to-consumer brands, the box is not a container. It is a touchpoint, a brand ambassador, and increasingly a piece of shareable content. Research by Dotcom Distribution found that 52% of D2C consumers say premium packaging makes them more likely to buy from a brand again, and 40% share photos of premium packaging on social media. Yet most packaging interiors — the inner cradles and inserts — remain anonymous grey foam, entirely at odds with the premium outer box.

Mycelium interiors change this equation fundamentally. The warm, natural tones, the soft texture, and the composting story give your customer something to talk about — and to photograph.
